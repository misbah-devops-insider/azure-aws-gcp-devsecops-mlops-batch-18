# 7. Correct Introduction Structure for DevOps Interview

Diagram gives structured introduction format.

---

# Professional Version

Example:

> I am Mungerilal, working with XYZ company from last 15 years, out of which last 7 years in DevSecOps as an architect.

This contains:

* Name
* Company
* Total experience
* Relevant domain experience
* Current role

---

# Internship Version

Example:

> Myself Mungerilal, working in DevOps Insiders as an intern from last 1 year.

This works for fresher / learner storytelling.

---

# Then Add Customer Context

Very important next sentence:

> Currently I am engaged with a US-based customer and the use case for the customer is...

This transitions smoothly into project story.
