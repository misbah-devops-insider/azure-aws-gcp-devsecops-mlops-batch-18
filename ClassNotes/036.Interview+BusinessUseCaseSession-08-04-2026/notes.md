It also teaches how to build strong interview storytelling using real-world industry examples.

# 2. Strong Introduction Structure for Interviews

Diagram suggests introduction must follow a sequence:

---

## Step 1 – Personal Introduction

Start with:

* Name
* Current company
* Total experience
* Relevant experience

Example:

> Hi, myself Shyam, working with XYZ company from last 8 years, having total experience 17 years, out of which last 8 years in DevSecOps.

---

## Step 2 – Functional Use Case

Immediately explain:

> What business problem customer is solving

Because interviewers first want:

> Why project exists?

---

## Step 3 – Team Structure

Mention:

* Frontend count
* Backend count
* Database engineers
* Architects

---

## Step 4 – Your Role

Only then explain:

> My role as DevSecOps engineer was...
